Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

A focused meeting notes app that respects your privacy, your wallet, and your time.

🔓

Free & open source

GPL-3.0 licensed. No subscription, no freemium limits, no vendor lock-in. Free forever.

🔒

Local transcription

Audio transcription runs on-device with whisper.cpp — your recordings never leave your Mac. Go fully offline with Ollama for summaries too.

💻

Native macOS app

Built with Swift and SwiftUI. Not Electron. Fast, lightweight, and right at home on macOS 14+.

🔑

Bring your own key

Use your own API key for OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini. Or run Ollama for a completely offline setup. No middleman, no markup.

📡

Smart meeting detection

Automatically detects when you join Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or Webex and starts recording.

📤

Flexible export

Send your notes to Apple Notes, Notion, or export as Markdown. Your data, your format.

Who it's for

Built for people who care

For privacy-conscious professionals

Your audio is transcribed locally with whisper.cpp — recordings never leave your Mac. No accounts, no telemetry, no data collection. Use Ollama to keep everything fully offline.

For developers

Native Swift and SwiftUI. Whisper.cpp with Metal GPU acceleration. Protocol-based architecture. Read the code, fork it, make it yours.

For anyone tired of paying for meeting notes

No subscriptions, no credit card, no 14-day trials. Free forever with unlimited history. Your meetings, your data.
